Prior-Report Errors

Why my earlier ledger said −$661 when reality is +$51

  1. Commission: $120 overstated — assumed $3/RT, broker charges $1/RT on MES.
  2. Fill-based exit computation — used current_stop as exit price for ghost/EOD/WIN trades where state had no stop_fill_price. Broker's RealizedProfitLoss is the only source of truth and was not being queried.
  3. Portal ≠ broker on basis-drift days. Portal tracks SPX; broker realizes MES. Mar 30 and Apr 21 alone = $450 of gap that isn't visible in the DB's outcome_pnl column.
