The Problem

Longs on Gap-Up Days Are Losers

When SPX gaps up >30 pts at open, the move has already happened. Longs are chasing into resistance — buying the top of an overnight gap that institutional sellers are fading.

Backtest found 112 long trades on gap-up days with only 38% win rate and -290.9 pts of losses.

Design Choice

The filter is all-day, not just first 30 minutes. Gap-up resistance persists throughout the entire session because institutional sellers use the elevated level to distribute all day. Early morning entry (09:30-10:00) is the worst, but even afternoon longs on gap-up days underperform.
