Vanna Pivot Bounce — Setup Study

0DTE Alpha · 2026-04-22 · 40 historical trades since Mar 1 2026

How VPB works

VPB fires when three conditions stack:

  • Dominant vanna level exists — a strike with ≥12% concentration of absolute vanna value
  • Recent CVD divergence — price made a swing high/low while delta diverged (exhaustion pattern on MES range bars)
  • Spot within 15 pts of a dominant vanna strike

Once all three align, the setup places:

  • ENTRY at current spot, fading toward the vanna pivot
  • Target (TP) = 10 pts in trade direction
  • Stop (SL) = 8 pts against
  • Trail disabled — fixed 10/8 bracket

Direction logic:

  • POSITIVE vanna near spot → LONG (fade pullback into support magnet)
  • NEGATIVE vanna near spot → SHORT (fade bounce into resistance)

Community mechanics (from Volland research)

Per Volland Discord research (35k+ messages) and backtested Butterfly setup data:

Vanna signRoleCommunity-backtested WRMechanism
POSITIVE (GREEN) Magnet 72.7% WR (butterfly) Dealers LONG vanna, hedge INTO strike → price pulled toward level
NEGATIVE (RED) Repellent / break accelerator 18.8% WR (butterfly) Dealers SHORT vanna, hedge AWAY from strike → price pushed away; if broken, flips sign past strike → cascade acceleration

Key quote (BigBill, Mar 31): "Negative vanna above spot = potential breakout if things can get moving higher. It flips when you pass the strike."

Wizard of Ops: "I look at short-term vanna if I'm making a 0DTE thesis, but doesn't have as much weight as 0DTE Delta Decay."
