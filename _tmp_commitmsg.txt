gex_long_v3: commit per iteration in _build_cache (root cause of 2026-06-03 outage)

raw_connection() is non-autocommit, so the hourly portal overlay rebuild ran
as ONE transaction holding AccessShareLock on chain_snapshots and
volland_exposure_points for minutes. During a deploy, the old instance's
rebuild blocked the new instance's db_init ALTER -> statement timeout ->
crash loop (Application failed to respond). Diagnosis credit: VPS session.

Reads only, so a commit at the top of each lid iteration just ends the txn
and releases locks - max hold time drops from minutes to sub-seconds.
