Risk catalog: edge cases where architecture proposal would fail

  • Bot offline (Railway redeploy/crash) — SPX-trail signal can't fire if poller dead. Broker hard SL still protects but trail not advanced. Mitigation: heartbeat alert on SPX-poller, fallback to MES-trail if poller >2 cycles late.
  • 30s SPX poll vs sub-second MES move — In a true flash crash, SPX can drop 20+ pts between 30s polls. Broker hard SL at 14pt is your capital backstop, fine, but missed trail-lock opportunity if 20pt drop happens in 30s window.
  • chain_snapshots is 2-min not 30s in V14 era — even worse latency than assumed 30s. Means SPX-driven trail will lag MES wicks by 1-2 min. To make this proposal viable, would need to switch chain_snapshots polling back to 30s.
  • SPX gap below trail level — if SPX gaps through the trail level (rare intraday but possible at open), broker SL would catch it sooner than SPX-trail. Net: SPX-trail might lose 2-3pt more on the gap. Mitigation: floor SPX exit at broker SL level.
  • SIM trade differs from real trade — the architecture-fix sim above assumes "market exit at SPX trigger" happens cleanly. In real life, between SPX poll detecting trigger and bot sending market order, MES could have wicked another 1-2pt. Net effect: arch fix captures ~80% of theoretical gain, not 100%.
