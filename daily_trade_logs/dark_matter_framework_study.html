8 weekly plans (Apr 13 → Jun 8 2026) · validated vs our full-history DB (Feb–Jun, 3,568 outcomes) · for Faisal
The whole thing in one paragraph
He never bets a direction — he reads the dealer/vol regime first, then trades the matching mode. In low-vol "positive-gamma" weeks (7 of his 8) he does range / buy-dips / fade-rips — literally what our system already does, and those are the weeks we make money. In the one EXTREME-vol "amplification" week (6/8) he flips to short / fade-rallies — the only week we bled. The data proves the mechanism: in high-vol regimes the short side roughly quadruples. Our gap is that we have no regime switch and almost no short supply when vol flips. Building that switch is the edge.

2 · How he sees the market (plain English)

Every plan is built in the same order — control first, direction last:

Layer 1 — Regime (the master switch)
  • Spot-vol beta: spot & vol moving together → dealers reactive → moves amplify. Normal → dealers in control → mean-revert.
  • Gamma sign: positive = pin/chop (fade both ways); negative = trend (ranges extend, levels break).
  • Vol vs its 6-mo baseline: above ~90th pct = EXTREME → polarity inverts (vanna above spot becomes resistance, not magnet). 6/8: IV 21.22 vs 17.58 → EXTREME.
  • Catalyst window: CPI/FOMC/NFP/geopolitics override — "dealer flow is secondary till the dust settles."
Layer 2 — Levels (where, not which way): Volland gamma/vanna/charm walls overlaid on volume-profile value areas + prior-week highs/lows, tagged by term-stack [0][W][M][L] (quad-stack = strongest), weighted by vol+catalyst.
Layer 3 — Bias = a decision tree, never fixed: "above X range A↔B; below X waterfall to C," entered only on a 15-min rejection/hold confirmation at the level. He never front-runs. This confirmation step is why his discretion beats a blind mechanical gate. Half-size in mixed/EXTREME regimes.
