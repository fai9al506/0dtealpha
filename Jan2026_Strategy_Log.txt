    - Trigger: when stock price drops to -GEX minus 1%
    - The call is OTM at purchase (stock below strike = cheap)
    - Delta at entry: ~0.35-0.45 (OTM)

  TARGETS:
    T1 = -GEX level (stock recovers to strike = call goes ATM)
         Stock move: +1.0%  |  Option: +200-800%
    T2 = +GEX magnet (stock reaches magnet = call ITM)
         Stock move: +1-3%  |  Option: +800-2500%

  EXIT:
    - T2 hit: SELL immediately (take full profit)
    - T1 hit but not T2: SELL (take profit, don't hold for T2)
    - Friday close: SELL whatever value remains (usually loss)
    - No stock stop needed. Max loss = premium paid.
