What shipped today (2 fixes)

#1 — Basket Gate / "Scheme B" DORMANT — arm Monday validated 3 months

What it does: before placing a real TSRT trade, it checks whether the big tech names (NVDA, AMD, AVGO, META, MSFT, GOOGL) are moving the same direction as the trade. If the basket contradicts (or is neutral), the trade is skipped. Only basket-confirmed trades are placed ("0/0/1").

Why it fixes the bleed: trades the basket confirms win 72%; trades it contradicts win only 54% — and that split held every month Mar→Jun. Skipping the contradicted trades removes most of the bad-week losses. On June, it skips the ~65% of trades that were fighting the tape.

Safety: single choke point in real_trader.place_trade() · fail-soft — any missing/stale data or error means the trade is taken (it can only skip, never add a loss) · env switch BASKET_GATE_ENABLED (default OFF — ships dormant) · new module app/basket_gate.py.

#2 — S131 Trail-Bug Fix LIVE on deploy post-V16 tested

What it does: in "ride-the-portal" (S131) mode, the trailing exit no longer fires on a jumpy MES tick — the smooth SPX path (check_spx_trail_exit) owns the exit, with the broker stop as the safety net.

Why it fixes the bleed: a known bug was kicking us out of winning trades early on an MES wiggle — e.g. trade #3905 booked +$6.8 on the broker vs +$25.3 in the portal. Fixing it lets winners run to the real (SPX) exit.

Safety: scoped to S131 internal-trail mode only (first-realign & non-S131 behavior unchanged) · broker disaster-stop always present · revert via SPX_EXIT_ENABLED=false · real_trader.update_stop().
